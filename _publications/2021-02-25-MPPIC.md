This paper compares two Eulerian-Lagrangian methods, CFD-DEM and MPPIC, for simulating biomass gasification in a fluidized bed. The two methods are evaluated against experimental data in terms of particle dynamics, gas composition and computational cost, providing guidance on choosing between particle-resolved and parcel-based approaches for gasifier simulation.
