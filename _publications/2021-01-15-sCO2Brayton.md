This paper compares three modified supercritical CO2 (sCO2) Brayton recompression cycles based on energy and exergy analysis. Each cycle configuration is optimized with a genetic algorithm (GA), and their thermal efficiency, exergy efficiency and component-level exergy destruction are analyzed to identify the most promising layout for power generation applications.
